    About Super Famicom

    Released in 1990 in Japan, the Super Famicom was Nintendo's second major home console, following the legendary Famicom released in 1983. The Super Famicom was released elsewhere in the world in 1991 (USA) and 1992 (Europe and Australia) as the Super Nintendo Entertainment System.

    Whatever name you choose for this legendary console, there's no denying that it left its mark on millions of gamers around the world. Today, the original Super Famicom is one of the easiest ways to explore the fascinating world of import gaming.

    Recommended Games

    Indispensable titles for the Super Famicom include Final Fantasy III, Super Mario World, Super Metroid, Super Castlevania IV, StarFox, F-Zero, Street Fighter II, Fatal Fury (released as Garou Densetsu in Japan), and Super Mario Kart.

    For what it's worth, my favorite Super Famicom game is Super Mario World II: Yoshi's Island.
